# Data Retention Policy (Draft)

> This document is a draft. Legal review will be completed before production launch.

## Learners

Data is retained while enrolled. After unenrollment, data is archived for approximately 90 days (exact window TBD) to allow export, then deleted.

## Teachers / organization staff

Data is retained indefinitely while the person's teacher/admin status is active within an organization. Deleted on request.

## Deleted data

Backups may retain deleted data for approximately 30 days (exact window TBD) after the primary record is deleted.

## Compliance

GDPR right-to-deletion is intended to be supported; implementation is deferred past MVP.
